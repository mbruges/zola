CALLOUS 🤖

Definition: showing or having an insensitive and cruel disregard for others

Etymology: The word 'callous' comes from the Middle English word 'callos', which means 'hardened' or 'toughened'.

Example Sentences:

1. His callous attitude towards the poor was disturbing.

2. She couldn't believe how callous her ex-boyfriend was after their break-up.

3. The soldier had a callous attitude towards the people he was there to protect.

Practice Tasks:

1. Rewrite each of the three example sentences, replacing the word callous with a synonym.

2. Write a short letter to the editor of your local newspaper describing a callous act you witnessed and explaining why it was wrong.

3. Create a dialogue between two characters, one of whom demonstrates a callous attitude towards the other.

Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story that includes the word callous.

Drawing Task:

Create a drawing that represents the meaning of the word callous.
