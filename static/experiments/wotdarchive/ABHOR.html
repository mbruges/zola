The Word of The Day is ABHOR 😭

Abhor: to regard with disgust or hatred

Etymology: from the Latin word abhorrere, meaning "to shrink from in horror"

Example Sentences:

  1. She abhors the thought of having to speak in public.
  2. The idea of eating sushi abhors him.
  3. He abhors the way his ex-girlfriend treated him.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Write a sentence about a time when you abhorred a food.
  2. Use the word abhor in a sentence about a person you strongly dislike.
  3. Write a sentence about something you abhor doing for school.

Creative Writing Task:

You wake up one morning to find that everyone in the world has disappeared except for one person - someone you abhor. Write a story about what happens next.

Drawing Task:

Draw a picture of something that you abhor.
