The Word of The Day is ALACRITY ✨

ALACRITY (noun): brisk and cheerful readiness

Etymology: The word derives from the Latin word alacritas, meaning eagerness.

Example Sentences:

  • He responded to the emergency with alacrity.
  • She showed alacrity in completing the task before the deadline.
  • His alacrity in volunteering for the project ensured its success.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Write a sentence using alacrity to describe someone's response to a request.
  2. Use alacrity in a sentence to describe completing a task efficiently.
  3. Write a sentence describing someone who lacks alacrity in their work.

Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story in which the protagonist must use alacrity to overcome an obstacle.

Drawing Task:

Draw a picture of a character who embodies alacrity.
