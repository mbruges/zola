About the PASS test

The PASS test is a survey used to measure how you feel about school. It is designed to help teachers and students identify areas where they may need additional support or guidance.

It isn't something you can revise for, and there are no right or wrong answers. Just answer honestly!

Having trouble finding your PASS code? Ask your teacher for help.
