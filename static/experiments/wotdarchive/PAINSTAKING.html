PAINSTAKING

A word that describes something that requires a lot of effort, diligence, and attention to detail.

Etymology

The word 'painstaking' originated from the combination of 'pain' meaning 'effort' and 'staking' meaning 'taking careful steps'.

Example Sentences:

  1. She completed the project with painstaking precision.
  2. Writing the essay was a painstaking task, but she managed to complete it in time.
  3. The artist's paintings are a result of his painstaking attention to detail.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Create a paragraph describing a painstaking process you have experienced.
  2. Write a sentence using the word 'painstaking' to describe a challenging task.
  3. Rewrite the following sentence using the word 'painstaking': "He carefully assembled the intricate model."

Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story about an individual who achieves great success through their painstaking efforts.

Drawing Task:

Using pen and paper, create a detailed drawing of an object or scene that showcases the concept of 'painstaking'.
