The Word of The Day is PALPABLE 👍

Definition: Palpable (adjective) ‚Äì capable of being touched or felt; easily perceived by the senses or the mind.

Etymology: The word "palpable" derives from the Latin word "palpabilis," meaning "that can be touched, felt, or handled."

Example Sentences:

  • The tension in the room was palpable as they awaited the final results.
  • His nervousness was palpable as he stepped onto the stage to give his speech.
  • Her disappointment was palpable when she didn't receive the promotion.
