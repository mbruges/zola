Definition: (adj.) wicked or criminal

Etymology: The word nefarious comes from the Latin word 'nefarious', which means 'not right' or 'flagrantly wicked'. It is derived from the word 'nefas' which means 'crime'.

Example sentences:

  1. She plotted a nefarious plan to rob the bank.
  2. His nefarious actions resulted in his arrest.
