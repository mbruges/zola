The Word of The Day is 😴LETHARGY

Definition:

LETHARGY is a state of extreme tiredness or lack of energy.

Etymology:

The word lethargy originated from the Late Latin lethargia, which is derived from the Greek word lethargos meaning "forgetful" or "dull".

Example Sentences:

  • I couldn't shake off the feeling of lethargy after staying up all night.
  • His constant lack of motivation led to a state of chronic lethargy.
  • The humid weather made me feel sluggish and prone to lethargy.

Creative Writing Task:
