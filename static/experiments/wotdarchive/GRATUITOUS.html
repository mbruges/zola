The Word of The Day is 😄 GRATUITOUS

Definition: adjective - given or done without apparent reason or justification; unnecessary or uncalled for.

Etymology: The word "gratuitous" originated from the Latin word "gratuitus" meaning "given freely, spontaneous, voluntary."

Example Sentences:

  1. His speech was filled with gratuitous insults, causing a stir among the audience.
  2. I was disappointed by the movie's gratuitous violence, as it served no purpose in advancing the storyline.
  3. The company decided against hiring more employees due to the gratuitous costs that would bring.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Create a sentence using the word gratuitous in a positive context.
  2. Write a paragraph explaining why gratuitous advertisements can be harmful.
  3. Compose a short dialogue including the word gratuitous to illustrate its meaning.

Open-ended Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story that revolves around a character who constantly makes gratuitous gestures of kindness without expecting anything in return.

Drawing Task:

Draw a scene depicting an act of gratuitous generosity and compassion.
