The Word of The Day is PARADIGM 💡

Definition:

Paradigm is a typical example or pattern of something. It represents a set of assumptions, concepts, values, and practices that forms the framework for understanding a subject or a domain.

Example Sentences:

  • I was taught a different paradigm of problem-solving in my mathematics class.
  • He challenged the traditional paradigm of leadership and introduced new strategies.
  • Shifting to renewable energy sources requires a change in the paradigm of energy production.
