Rules of Consequences

A serious game for unserious people

General Rules

  • Write legibly
  • Skewer cruelly
  • Fold carefully
  • Pass clockwisely
  • Repeat rapidly

IMPORTANT: Always deny all knowledge of who wrote what.

  9. She gave...
  10. The consequence was...
  11. The world said...
