The Word of The Day is 😱 ABERRATION

Definition:

An aberration refers to a departure from what is normal, expected, or typical.

Etymology:

The word aberration originated in the early 17th century from the Latin word "aberratio," meaning a straying away.

Example Sentences:

  • Aberration in his behavior caused concern among his friends.
  • Her decision to quit her stable job was seen as an aberration by her family.
  • The scientist considered the unusual results as an experimental aberration.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Write a sentence that incorporates the word aberration.
  2. Explain a real-life situation where an aberration might occur.
  3. Create a story plot involving an aberration that changes a character's life.

Creative Writing Task:

Write a creative story using the word aberration as a central theme. Explore the consequences and impact of the aberration within your story.

Drawing Task:

Create a drawing depicting an aberration in nature, art, or everyday life.
