The Word of The Day is NADIR

Nadir is the lowest point of something, typically the point where it reaches rock bottom.

Etymology

The word nadir comes from Arabic nƒÅzir, meaning "opposite", and is derived from the phrase "nadir as-samt", which means "opposite to zenith" or "opposite to the direction in which the sun appears to move".

Example Sentences

  • After the success of her last movie, her career hit a nadir.
  • The team's performance reached a nadir when they lost to the worst-ranked team.
  • His popularity dipped to a nadir after the scandal.
