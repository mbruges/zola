🤪 Frivolous 🤪

adjective: not having any serious purpose or value

Etymology: The word frivolous comes from Latin frivolus, meaning "silly" or "trifling."

Example Sentences:

  • She was criticized for her frivolous spending habits.
  • His argument was dismissed as frivolous and lacking in evidence.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Write a sentence using frivolous.
  2. Synonym Practice: Find a synonym for frivolous.
  3. Antonym Practice: Find an antonym for frivolous.

Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story about a character who lives a life without any frivolous pursuits. Do they find fulfillment in their lifestyle, or do they feel like they're missing out on something?

Drawing Task:

Draw a picture of something that is the opposite of frivolous.
