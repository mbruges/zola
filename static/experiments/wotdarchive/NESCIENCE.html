The Word of The Day is NESCIENCE 🤔

Nescience is a noun that means lack of knowledge or ignorance.

Etymology Summary

The word nescience originated from the Latin word "nescientia", which means "not knowing". It entered the English language in the early 17th century.

Example Sentences

  • Despite his confidence, his nescience in mathematics was evident.
  • The teacher was patient with the student's nescience regarding the topic.
  • Her success was hindered by her nescience of the industry's latest trends.

Practice Tasks

Task 1

Task 2

Create a dialogue between two characters where one expresses frustration about the other's nescience.

Task 3

Compose a short paragraph describing a personal experience where you faced nescience and how you overcome it.

Creative Writing Task

Write a story using nescience as a central theme. Explore the consequences and impact of ignorance in the narrative.

Drawing Task

Create a visual representation of the word nescience. Use colors, symbols, and imagery to depict the concept of lack of knowledge or ignorance.
