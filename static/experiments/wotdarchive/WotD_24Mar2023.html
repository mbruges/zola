Definition: to defeat completely

Etymology: The word 'Vanquish' originated from Old French 'vainquir' which means 'to conquer' and from Latin 'vincere' which means 'to win'.

Example Sentences:

  1. He will vanquish his opponent in the upcoming debate.
  2. The army was able to vanquish the rebels after a long battle.

Practice Tasks

  1. Find five synonyms for vanquish.
  2. Create an antonym for vanquish.
  3. Write a sentence using vanquish in the passive voice.

Creative Writing Task

You are a hero on a quest to vanquish an evil sorcerer who has been terrorizing a kingdom. Write a short story describing your journey, the battle, and your victory.

Drawing Task

Draw a picture of a knight vanquishing a dragon.
