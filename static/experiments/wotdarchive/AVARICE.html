The Word of The Day is AVARICE 💰

Avarice: extreme greed for wealth or material gain.

Etymology: The word "avarice" comes from the Latin word "avaritia" meaning "greed".

Example Sentences:

  • His avarice for money led him down a dark path.
  • Peter's avarice caused him to cheat on the exam.
  • The CEO's avarice put the company's future in jeopardy.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Rewrite this sentence using the word "avarice": His extreme desire for power corrupts him.
  2. Use "avarice" in a sentence that describes a character in a story.
  3. Create a scenario where someone's avarice leads to negative consequences.

Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story about a person who overcomes their avarice.

Drawing Task:

Create a drawing that represents the negative effects of avarice.
