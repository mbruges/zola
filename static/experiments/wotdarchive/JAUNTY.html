The Word of The Day is JAUNTY 🎉

Definition:

A jaunty person or thing has a lively and cheerful manner or appearance.

Etymology:

The word jaunty originated from the French word genter, which means "to make elegant or well-groomed."

Example Sentences:

  1. I saw him walking down the street with a jaunty stride.
  2. She wore a jaunty hat that caught everyone's attention.
  3. The music had a jaunty rhythm that made everyone want to dance.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Create a sentence using jaunty to describe someone's personality.
  2. Write a short paragraph using at least two instances of the word jaunty.
  3. Compose a dialogue between two characters where one compliments the other's jaunty outfit.

Creative Writing Task:

Write a story about a character who discovers a magical object that grants them a jaunty ability.

Drawing Task:

Create a sketch of something or someone that embodies the essence of the word jaunty.
