🌞 The Word of The Day is BENEVOLENT 🌞

Definition: kind and generous

Etymology: from Latin bene (well) + volens (willing)

Example sentences:

  • The benevolent stranger gave me directions and offered me a ride.
  • The benevolent millionaire donated millions of dollars to charity.

Practice tasks:

  1. Find and highlight the benevolent actions in this story.
  2. Identify and replace the opposite of benevolent words in this paragraph.
  3. Write a sentence using benevolent to describe a person you admire.

Creative writing task:

Write a short story about a benevolent creature that helps people in need.

Drawing task:

Draw a picture of a benevolent character that you create.
