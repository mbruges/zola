The Word of The Day is PERUSAL 📙

Definition:

A perusal is the act of reading or examining something carefully and thoroughly.

Etymology Summary:

The word perusal originated from the Latin word "perusare," which means to thoroughly examine or survey.

Example Sentences:

  1. She took her time in perusal of the important document before making her decision.
  2. After a quick perusal of the menu, I decided to order the seafood pasta.
  3. The editor requested a perusal of the writer's latest manuscript.

Practice Tasks:

Task 1:

Task 2:

Task 3:
