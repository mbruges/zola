The Word of The Day is VORACIOUS 🦐

Voracious (adjective) - having a huge appetite; excessively eager

Etymology: From the Latin word "vorax" meaning "greedy"

Example sentences:

1. The voracious lion caught and devoured its prey in a matter of seconds.

2. She has a voracious appetite for reading books, and can finish one within a day.

Practice tasks:

  1. Write a sentence using voracious.
  2. Find a synonym for voracious.
  3. Create a sentence using the synonym you found.

Creative writing: Write a short story about a voracious monster that terrorizes a village.

Drawing task: Draw a picture of a voracious animal.
