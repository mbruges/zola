🌊 The Word of The Day is LIGUID

Definition:

LIGUID means a state of matter characterized by its ability to flow and take the shape of its container.

Etymology:

The word LIGUID originated from the Latin word "liquere" which means "to be fluid".

Example Sentences:

  1. LIGUID water poured from the bottle into the glass.
  2. The painter mixed various LIGUID colors to create a beautiful artwork.
  3. When ice melts, it turns into a LIGUID state.
